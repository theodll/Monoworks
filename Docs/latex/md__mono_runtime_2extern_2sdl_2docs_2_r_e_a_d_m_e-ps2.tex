If you trying to debug a SDL app through \href{https://github.com/ps2dev/ps2client}{\texttt{ps2client}} you need to avoid the IOP reset, otherwise you will lose the connection with your computer. So to avoid the reset of the IOP CPU, you need to call to the macro {\ttfamily SDL\+\_\+\+PS2\+\_\+\+SKIP\+\_\+\+IOP\+\_\+\+RESET();}. It could be something similar as:\+ 
\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{.....}
\DoxyCodeLine{}
\DoxyCodeLine{SDL\_PS2\_SKIP\_IOP\_RESET();}
\DoxyCodeLine{}
\DoxyCodeLine{\textcolor{keywordtype}{int}\ main(\textcolor{keywordtype}{int}\ argc,\ \textcolor{keywordtype}{char}\ *argv[])}
\DoxyCodeLine{\{}
\DoxyCodeLine{.....}

\end{DoxyCode}
 For a release binary is recommendable to reset the IOP always.
